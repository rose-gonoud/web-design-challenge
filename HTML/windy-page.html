Wind Speed vs. X

It seems that wind speeds are lowest at the equator, and start to drift up as you approach latitudes further out. The effect is subtle, though, so it's almost certainly not statistically significant even for this one day and furthermore may not remain observable across many days. Wind speeds probably do not trend much with latitude.
