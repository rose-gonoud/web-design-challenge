Cloudiness vs. X

Overall, cloudiness levels seem clustered around 0 and 100, with fewer data points in between. There is also clear data clustering at measurements that are multiples of 5 and 10. This may say more about human rounding tendencies when collecting this data than it does about the actual spread of clouds around the globe.

Overall this data does not contain any significant trend based on location. While certain values still cluster, they don't cluser along geographic location very strongly.
