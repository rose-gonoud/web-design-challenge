Humidity vs. X

This analysis of % humidity variation across latitudes shows that, on 1/29/2020, the greatest overall variance in humidity occured in cities between 0 and 20 degrees latitude. Also, from 40 degrees onward, humidities seem overall much higher than within any other latitude range.

Overall, however, this data does not contain any significant trend based on location. While certain values still cluster, they don't cluser along geographic location very strongly.
